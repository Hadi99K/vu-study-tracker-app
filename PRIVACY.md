# Privacy Policy for VU Study Tracker

*Last updated: December 11, 2025*

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to VU Study Tracker. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have complete control over your data. This Privacy Policy explains how our application handles your information.

## 2. Data Collection and Storage
**Local Storage Only**: VU Study Tracker is a local-first application. All data you enter—including course details, grades, study schedules, and notes—is stored securely on your own device.

**No External Servers**: We do not upload, sync, or transmit your personal academic data to any external cloud servers or third-party databases. You maintain full ownership and custody of your data at all times.

## 3. Permissions
To provide specific features, we may request the following permissions on your device:
*   **Notifications**: To send you study reminders and exam alerts.
*   **Storage/File Access**: To allow you to attach and save PDF course materials.

## 4. Data Deletion
Since your data resides solely on your device, deleting the application will permanently erase all your stored information. We recommend exporting any critical data (if the feature is available) before uninstalling the app.
